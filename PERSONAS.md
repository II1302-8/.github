# DockPulse Personas

These are the user roles we write stories for. When writing a user story, use one of these, never "As a user...".

## Visiting boater

A boater arriving at the harbor looking for a place to dock. Does not have a long-term berth assignment. Wants to find a free berth quickly, ideally before walking the dock.

**Cares about:** berth availability, location, booking a spot, clear on-site signage.

## Harbor master

Staff member responsible for harbor operations. On duty during the day, often on a tablet walking the docks.

**Cares about:** live occupancy overview, unauthorized mooring alerts, node health, provisioning new sensors, responding to issues fast.

## Berth tenant

A boat owner with a long-term berth assignment. Sometimes leaves for days or weeks and wants their berth used by visitors while away.

**Cares about:** marking berth available, setting return date, confirming their slot is free when they come back.

## Harbor maintenance

Staff member responsible for the physical sensor network. May not be on site daily.

**Cares about:** battery levels, offline nodes, replacing failed hardware, firmware updates.
